The fabulous rubycorns

Who?

We are a study group meeting on Tuesdays to learn ruby, rails and other things programming.

Right now people with various skill levels meet and work on things related to code. If you're a person who identifies as a woman, you're welcome to come by and work on things as well.

What?

We maintain and constantly try to improve rorganize.it an app for learners to find groups, coaches and other learners. Check the code on GitHub.

We also started JuniorsAreAwesome (which is no longer active at this point) and whatdojuniorsgetpaid (which is not yet active at this point). Check them out anyway!

There are also other learning materials provided by our fellow study group the RubyMonstas and other tutorials.

No Ruby? No problem! We also hack on JavaScript, Elixir and ops things. Or anything really.

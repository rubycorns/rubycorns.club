The fabulous rubycorns

Who?

We are a study group meeting on Tuesdays to learn ruby, rails and other things programming.

Right now people with various skill levels meet and work on things and you're welcome to come by and work on things as well.

What?

We maintain and constantly try to improve rorganize.it an app for other Rails Girls to find groups, coaches and other students. Check the code on GitHub.

There are also other learning materials provided by our fellow study group the RubyMonstas and other tutorials.
